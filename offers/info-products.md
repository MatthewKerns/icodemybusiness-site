Internal reference for digital info products available for purchase.

## Product Ideas & Pipeline

### Ready to Build

#### 1. The Organic Marketing Playbook
- **Format:** PDF guide / digital download
- **Price range:** $27–$47
- **Content:** Step-by-step system for replacing paid ads with organic traffic
- **Covers:**
  - TikTok content automation (elemental strategy)
  - AI Assistant Optimization (AEO) basics
  - Email capture with gamified mechanics
  - Blog content engine setup
- **Audience:** E-commerce brand owners spending $2K+/month on ads

#### 2. AI Agent Blueprint Templates
- **Format:** Digital download — templates + walkthrough guide
- **Price range:** $19–$37
- **Content:** Reusable Claude Code plan templates for business and wellness
- **Includes:**
  - Business Management OS template
  - Personal Wellness System template
  - Data integration architecture template
  - Customization guide
- **Audience:** Technical founders, solopreneurs, AI enthusiasts

#### 3. Financial Health Tracking Blueprint
- **Format:** PDF + spreadsheet/Notion template
- **Price range:** $17–$29
- **Content:** The same blueprint currently offered as a free bonus with wellness discovery calls
- **Covers:**
  - Daily financial pulse tracking
  - Monthly budget automation
  - Net worth dashboard setup
  - AI-powered expense categorization
- **Audience:** Wellness-minded professionals, personal finance enthusiasts

#### 4. TikTok Elemental Strategy Guide
- **Format:** PDF + video walkthrough
- **Price range:** $37–$67
- **Content:** The 4-channel elemental strategy used for Infinity Vault
- **Covers:**
  - Air (ethereal), Water (flow), Fire (bold), Earth (grounded) channel setup
  - Content calendar for 19+ videos/week
  - Save rate optimization tactics
  - Algorithm insights and posting schedule
- **Audience:** E-commerce brands, content creators, TikTok Shop sellers

## Pricing Strategy

| Product | Price | Upsell Path |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| AI Agent Blueprint Templates | $19–$37 | → Discovery call → Consulting |
| Financial Health Blueprint | $17–$29 | → Wellness discovery call |
| Organic Marketing Playbook | $27–$47 | → Free audit → Managed execution |
| TikTok Elemental Strategy | $37–$67 | → Free audit → Full automation |

**Philosophy:** Info products serve as low-friction entry points. They deliver real value on their own but naturally lead buyers toward discovery calls and consulting.
